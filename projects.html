Skincare Recommendation System

Made with: Python, HuggingFace

February 2025 - Ongoing

A machine learning-powered skincare assistant that personalizes skincare routines and recommends beauty products based on user surveys. The app allows users to customize their routines while providing tailored product suggestions.

×

I'm leading the machine learning development for this project. My work began with web scraping skincare data, including product names, ingredients, and allergens, and transforming it into a structured JSON file. Currently, I'm focused on feeding this data into HuggingFace to convert it into vectors. This will enable comparison searches using prompt engineering, ultimately providing optimal product recommendations based on a user's skin conditions. Meanwhile, our team is integrating the frontend and backend to deploy our skincare recommendation app.

Arduino PCR COVID-19 Detector

Made with: C/C++, Arduino IDE

Completed: June 2022

A COVID-19 detection tool that uses a computer algorithm to simulate the PCR process and identify the virus by analyzing genetic information.

×

I developed an algorithm that computationally simulates the Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) process, a fundamental technique in molecular biology for amplifying target DNA sequences. The model replicates the thermal cycling stages of PCR (denaturation, annealing, and extension) to mimic the DNA duplication process. After amplification, the algorithm applies sequence alignment methods to compare the synthesized DNA segments against a database of known viral genomes. This enables the identification of specific viral strains based on their genetic markers, allowing for the detection of the COVID-19 virus in DNA samples.
