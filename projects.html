Arduino PCR COVID-19 Detector



I designed and implemented an algorithm that computationally simulates the Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) process, a key technique used in molecular biology to amplify target DNA sequences. My model mimics the thermal cycling stages of PCR, including denaturation, annealing, and extension, to replicate how DNA strands are duplicated. Once multiplied, the algorithm uses sequence alignment techniques to compare the resulting DNA segments against a database of known viral genomes, enabling the identification of specific viral strains based on their genetic markers.
